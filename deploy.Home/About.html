About this Site

Artwork

I’ve decorated this site with some of my favorite art. This includes some pieces done by my sister, Rachel Bracha, as well as some famous masterpieces. Due to the repressive intellectual property climate, I’ve stuck to either classics that are in the public domain, or work that I could easily get permission to use. A lot of things in between are just too much trouble to deal with.

Background

Originally, the black background was chosen to minimize the radiation on a CRT. CRTs are a relic now, but I still like it.

This site was built using Apple’s iWeb tool.
